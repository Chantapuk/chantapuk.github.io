Our Story

From humble beginnings to Bangkok's favorite bakery

Founded in 2010, Sweet Gluttony began as a small family-owned bakery with a simple mission: to create delicious, high-quality baked goods that bring joy to our community. Our journey started with just three signature items - our classic croissant, sourdough bread, and chocolate cake.

Today, we've grown into a beloved local institution, but our commitment to quality and traditional baking methods remains unchanged.

Our Values

Quality

We use only the finest ingredients and traditional baking methods to ensure exceptional taste and quality.

Community

We're proud to be part of our local community and strive to create a welcoming environment for all.

Sustainability

We're committed to sustainable practices and reducing our environmental impact.
